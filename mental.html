ABOUT MENTAL HEALTH

Mental health includes our emotional, behavioural and social well-being. To have a good physical health it is very important that one has normal and healthy mind. However, the stressful and hectic modern lifestyle increases risk of having poor Mental Health. Mental illnesses such as depression, anxiety or sleep problems are seen very commonly among people who have a very unhealthy lifestyle that includes poor diet, lack of physical activity, lack of social engagement and too much stress. It is a well-known fact that mental stress can eventually have some serious effects on our physical health.

SYMPTOMS OF MENTAL HEALTH
  • Feeling sad or down.
  • Confused thinking or reduced ability to concentrate.
  • Excessive fears or worries, or extreme feelings of guilt.
  • Extreme mood changes of highs and lows.
  • Withdrawal from friends and activities.

CAUSES OF MENTAL HEALTH
  • childhood abuse, trauma, or neglect.
  • social isolation or loneliness.
  • experiencing discrimination and stigma, including racism.
  • social disadvantage, poverty or debt.
